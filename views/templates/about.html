About the developer
After starting a career as a pharmacist, I decided to switch, because I wasn't feeling satisfied about what I'm doing, I needed something exciting where i can work really hard and be creative. So I had the chance to access one of the biggest national school in my country to become a computer science Engineer that can develop, paticularly in Web developement. I'm working actually with Pasteur Laboratory in Tunisia (Tunis) to develop a web application, where we analyze the data issued from the RNA sequencing of the Leishmania parasite, and compare it with the data present on the databases, to check on the possibilities of finding new species depending on their RNA-seq outputs. I'm very passionate about creating new tools and services which can help in a way, other people. I want to be a part of that large community of developers who were able to change many people's life, and make it a better place.
